# Quality Rubric

Score `DESIGN.md` out of 100.

## Scoring

### Product specificity: 20

- 0 to 5: generic SaaS adjectives
- 6 to 12: partially specific
- 13 to 20: clearly shaped by product, user, and situation

### Visual point of view: 15

- 0 to 5: no real Creative North Star
- 6 to 10: named but weakly applied
- 11 to 15: clear visual thesis that affects layout, components, and copy

### System completeness: 20

- 0 to 7: missing major sections
- 8 to 14: sections exist but lack implementation detail
- 15 to 20: clear enough for a developer or AI agent to implement

### Anti-slop strength: 15

- 0 to 5: generic warnings
- 6 to 10: concrete forbidden patterns
- 11 to 15: forbidden patterns plus replacements

### Accessibility and localization: 10

- 0 to 3: weak or absent
- 4 to 7: general accessibility notes
- 8 to 10: concrete contrast, focus, text, motion, RTL, and input rules

### Component usefulness: 15

- 0 to 5: components named only
- 6 to 10: partial state and anatomy rules
- 11 to 15: component rules cover variants, states, anatomy, and behavior

### Agent readiness: 5

- 0 to 2: human-readable only
- 3 to 5: includes clear guardrails for implementation agents

## Pass bar

Minimum acceptable score: 80.

Below 80, revise before implementation.
